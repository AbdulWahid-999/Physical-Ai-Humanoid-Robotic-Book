# Introduction to Physical AI

Physical AI is an emerging field that combines artificial intelligence with robotics to create intelligent systems that can interact with the real world. This textbook explores the fundamentals of physical AI and humanoid robotics, providing a comprehensive guide for students and enthusiasts.

## Key Concepts

- Embodied AI
- Robotics
- Sensorimotor control
- Human-robot interaction

## Applications

Physical AI has a wide range of applications, including:

- Autonomous navigation
- Industrial automation
- Healthcare robotics
- Service robots

This chapter will delve into these concepts and applications, laying the groundwork for understanding the complexities of building intelligent robots.
